03 — Bachelor Thesis

Loewe-Inspired
Tableware Prototype

Independent academic concept project. Not affiliated with or commissioned by the brand.

Year

2024

Type

Academic Concept

Role

Concept · Brand Decoding · Prototype Design

Context

Bachelor Thesis

Concept

A speculative tableware collection inspired by Loewe's craftsmanship language, sculptural forms, and tactile material focus — designed to function both as dining object and display artifact. The project explores how a luxury fashion house's DNA can be coherently translated into functional objects without losing the brand's essential character.

loewe-detail-1.jpg — 800×600px
loewe-detail-2.jpg — 800×600px

Process

  • Analyzed Loewe brand DNA: craft heritage, leather materiality, sculptural silhouettes, and artisanal ethos.
  • Translated brand codes into a coherent form language for functional object design.
  • Explored materials, finishes, and production feasibility within luxury manufacturing contexts.
  • Developed CMF (color, material, finish) board with clear design rationale.
  • Designed user scenarios placing objects within a luxury hosting context.

Deliverables

Research deck · Concept sketches · Material board · Prototype renders

Skills Demonstrated

Brand Analysis Product Thinking Luxury Positioning CMF Development Concept Storytelling Material Research
