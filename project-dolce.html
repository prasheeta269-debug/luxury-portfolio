01 — Concept Study

Dolce&Gabbana
Editorial Tablescape Campaign

Speculative editorial concept created for a university assignment. Independent academic work, not commissioned by the brand.

Year

2024

Type

Concept Study

Role

Creative Direction · Styling · Shot Planning

Context

University Assignment

Context

University assignment to create an imagined editorial campaign translating Dolce&Gabbana brand codes into a styled tablescape narrative designed for photography.

Concept

An editorial table moment inspired by Dolce&Gabbana's dramatic Italian romanticism — strong contrast, sensual materiality, ornate references, and visual intensity — composed to translate powerfully in both wide frames and intimate detail shots.

dolce-detail-1.jpg — 800×600px
dolce-detail-2.jpg — 800×600px

Process

  • Researched Dolce&Gabbana brand codes including Italian heritage, baroque drama, sensual silhouettes, and decorative motifs.
  • Built a defined color and material palette to reflect brand identity.
  • Developed moodboards and styling direction with clear compositional hierarchy.
  • Designed multiple layout variations for different camera perspectives (hero shot, overhead, detail sequences).
  • Curated tableware, linens, candles, and decorative elements with editorial intention.
  • Structured a shotlist to ensure visual storytelling across frames.
  • Refined styling for balance, scale, contrast, and texture.

Deliverables

Creative rationale · Moodboard · Styling plan · Shotlist · Final images

Skills Demonstrated

Editorial Styling Brand Translation Concept Development Composition Visual Hierarchy Luxury Storytelling
